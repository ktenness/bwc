About

Bike Walnut Creek is a non-profit social organization founded in 2012 as a local working group of Bike East Bay.

Our vision is to create a comfortable and safe environment for bicyclists of all ages and abilities.

We do this by providing free services to bicyclists and advocating the city to create usable spaces for bicyclists.

Counts

Twice a year our amazing volunteers count bicyclists and pedestrians on city streets. The most recent bike counts for 42 location over the last 5 years are displayed in the map below. The larger the circle, the more bicyclists counted. The information collected tells us important information such as where to find the majority of bicyclists (on trails) and the gender distribution of bicyclists (data not shown).
