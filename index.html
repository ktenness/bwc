About

Bike Walnut Creek is a non-profit social organization founded in 2012 as a local working group of Bike East Bay.

Our vision is to create a comfortable and safe environment for bicyclists of all ages and abilities.

We do this by providing free services to bicyclists and advocating the city to create usable spaces for bicyclists.
